## Ingredients

I took 1.5 lbs of boneless, skinless, chicken thighs.

Spices:  add equal parts

* turmeric
* chili powder
* garlic salt

Also chopped olives and 1/2 cup chicken broth.

## Directions

Instant Pot Manual Pressure High for 9 minutes.
